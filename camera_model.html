How do I choose the right camera model?

Select the brand, then choose the model name that matches your camera body. You can also search by series or model number.

Does shutter count affect the resale price?

Yes. Shutter count, body condition, lens condition, battery health and accessories can affect the final quote.

Can I sell only the camera body?

Yes, you can sell only the camera body. Adding original lens, charger, battery, box or bill may improve the value.

Is doorstep pickup free?

Yes, doorstep pickup is free for supported locations. The final quote is confirmed after inspection.

Can I sell a damaged camera?

Yes. Share the actual condition during the selling process so the price can be calculated accordingly.

When will I get payment?

Payment is processed after successful verification and final price confirmation during pickup.
